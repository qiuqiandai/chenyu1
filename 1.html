Creating Characters

Origin: 10 initial career, according to personal preferences, the game can be developed into their favorite career by adding points. Knight class is the smoothest one among all classes, and other classes need a little bit of requirements for early operation.

Basic Operation

Ls: control the person to move [when you press LS during the running process, you can jump, jog and walk slowly to approach the enemy and use it in the game] LB: left hand attack or defense LT: left hand heavy attack or anti anti attack Rs: View move, press rs to lock enemy RB: right hand light attack【 Press RB back stab behind the enemy, push ls+rb kickshield forward to break defense, and cut down RB while falling] RT: right hand attack【 Push ls+rt split forward]

Weapons System

Physics: weapon panel attack power + correction attack power. The actual damage is affected by the enemy's defense attribute. Magic to dark attribute attack: weapon's additional attribute damage, panel attack + correction attack, actual damage is affected by enemy's defense attribute.
